Pre-Call Preparation

Review Borrower Information

  • Check application form for basic details (name, loan amount, loan type)
  • Review credit score and any flags
  • Note employment status and income level
  • Check if secured (car/vehicle) or unsecured (personal) loan
  • Review any previous communication notes
  • Identify any special circumstances (self-employed, multiple income sources, etc.)

Understand the Loan Request

  • Loan amount and purpose
  • For vehicle loans: vehicle age and estimated value
  • For vehicle loans: buying privately or from a dealer
  • For vehicle loans: do they have a vehicle already (trade-in potential)
  • Deposit amount available (if applicable)
  • Preferred loan term
  • Any time constraints or urgency

Check What's Missing

  • Identify which documents are outstanding
  • Note any incomplete information in application
  • Check if previous follow-ups have been attempted

Call Goals & Objectives

Primary Goals

  • Build rapport and trust with the customer
  • Obtain bank statements (last 3 months)
  • Obtain photo ID (driver's license or passport)
  • Confirm accuracy of application information

Secondary Goals

  • Understand customer's timeline and urgency
  • Identify any concerns or questions they have
  • Set expectations for next steps
  • Schedule follow-up if needed

Basic Call Scripts

Opening Script

"Hi [Customer Name], this is [Your Name] calling from MatchMe Money. How are you today? I'm calling about your loan application. Is now a good time to chat for a few minutes?"

If not a good time:

"No problem at all. When would be a better time for me to call you back?"

Building Rapport

"Thanks for choosing MatchMe Money to help with your [loan type - car loan/personal loan]. I can see you're looking at [vehicle type/loan purpose] - that sounds great!"
"I'm here to make this process as smooth as possible for you. Have you been through the lending process before, or is this your first time?"

For vehicle loans, ask:

"Just to confirm, are you buying from a dealer or privately? And do you currently have a vehicle that you might be trading in?"

Requesting Documents

Bank Statements:

"To move forward with your application, I'll need your bank statements from the last 3 months. This helps us understand your financial position and get you the best possible deal. Can you email those through to customer@matchmemoney.co.nz?"

Photo ID:

"I'll also need a copy of your photo ID - either your driver's license or passport. You can send that to the same email address: customer@matchmemoney.co.nz"

If they're hesitant:

"I completely understand wanting to be careful with your documents. We're registered financial advisers and all your information is kept strictly confidential. These documents are standard requirements from all lenders."

Confirming Information

"I just want to confirm a few details from your application to make sure everything's accurate..."
  • Confirm employment and income
  • Verify loan amount and deposit (if applicable)
  • For vehicle loans: confirm vehicle details, age, value, and purchase method
  • Check contact details are correct

Addressing Concerns

If they're shopping around:

"That's absolutely fine - it's smart to compare options. What I can tell you is that we work with multiple lenders, so we can often find better rates than going direct. Once you send through your documents, I can give you a clear picture of what we can offer."

If they're worried about approval:

"Let me have a look at what we can do. Every situation is different, and we specialize in finding solutions. Send through your documents and I'll give you an honest assessment of your options."

Closing Script

"Great, so just to recap - you'll email your bank statements and photo ID to customer@matchmemoney.co.nz, and I'll review everything and give you a call back within [timeframe]. Do you have any questions for me?"
"Perfect. If you think of anything before then, feel free to call us on 0800 696 282 or email that same address. Thanks for your time today, [Customer Name]."

If Voicemail

"Hi [Customer Name], this is [Your Name] from MatchMe Money calling about your loan application. I'd love to have a quick chat about next steps. Could you give me a call back on 0800 696 282, or reply to our email at customer@matchmemoney.co.nz? Thanks, and I look forward to speaking with you soon."

Post-Call Actions

Immediate Follow-Up

  • Send confirmation email summarizing what was discussed
  • List documents needed with clear deadline
  • Provide contact details again
  • Set reminder for follow-up if documents not received

CRM Notes

  • Record call outcome and customer responses
  • Note any concerns or special circumstances
  • Update application status
  • Schedule next action or follow-up date

Tips for Success

  • Plan your call and set your goals - be prepared before you dial
  • Be friendly but professional - you're a trusted advisor
  • Listen actively - let customers express concerns
  • Be patient - many customers are stressed about lending
  • Show expertise - but avoid jargon
  • Set clear expectations - be honest about timelines
  • Follow up promptly - build trust through reliability
